About Me

My name is Patience Maame Efua Agiri Andoh, a senior at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Kumasi, Ghana, studying Biological Sciences. Passionate about cancer biology and science education, I aim to make biology more relatable and impactful, especially for underserved communities.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, listening to Afrobeat music, and debating. As a Gemini born on May 30th, I believe in curiosity, communication, and duality. Through education and outreach, I strive to inspire a more health-literate and science-informed generation.
