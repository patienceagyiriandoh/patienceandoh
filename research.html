Jul. 2024 – Scientific Research Capstone Project

Department of Theoretical and Applied Biology, KNUST
Topic: Impacts of Mercury Exposure on Human Health

  • Synthesized peer-reviewed studies of 343 gold miners; identified 46.6% exposed to mercury
  • Used Chi-square & bivariate analysis from literature to connect mercury with numbness & rashes
  • Recommended interventions: PPE training, public health campaigns
  • Presented poster and defended results, earning an A in Scientific Research & Communication

Feb. 2023 – Microbiology Project

L.A. Ofori Group, Department of Theoretical and Applied Biology, KNUST
Topic: Ubiquitous Presence of Bacteria: An Environmental Exploration

  • Sterilized 40 petri dishes with Nutrient and Potato Dextrose Agar using 70% ethanol
  • Sampled lab air, outdoor air, soil, and plants — 100% of plates showed microbial growth
  • Identified Actinomyces, Staphylococcus, and Aspergillus, confirming environmental diversity
