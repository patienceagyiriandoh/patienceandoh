Cancer Biology Series

What is Cancer? — Abnormal cells invading tissue or metastasizing to other organs.

Hallmarks: Growth signaling, immune evasion, resisting cell death, angiogenesis, and metastasis.

Topics Covered:

  • Cell Cycle & Regulation (Cyclins, CDKs, checkpoints)
  • DNA Damage & Repair (BER, NER, MMR, HR, NHEJ)
  • Oncogenes vs Tumor Suppressors (RAS, TP53, BRCA)
